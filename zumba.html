Zumba (Zumba Fitness)

Zumba is not only a fiery dance. This is a well-planned workout that works out muscle groups of the whole body! Zumba dancers become more relaxed, graceful and agile.

Zumba (Zumba Fitness): training

Fitness zumba is the very sports and dance direction that helps you look and feel good, while expressing yourself in dance. Rhythmic Latin American music, a positive atmosphere has attracted the attention of millions of people from all over the world. Her fan base continues to grow, attracting more women and men. The main features of the Zumba direction The direction should be perceived precisely as a bright but effective fitness program used for weight loss. Basic movements include elements of several independent styles: samba, hip-hop, salsa, even flamenco. During the lesson, dancers use all muscle groups with varying degrees of intensity.

What is the use of zumba

Zumba Fitness lessons have many-sided health benefits. The most significant results should be highlighted: Improving the work of the vascular system, heart. Strengthens all muscles in the body, especially the back and abdomen. Intensive fat burning. Increased endurance. Energetic music creates a positive emotional mood. You can even dance zumba at home, honing your skill. Exercise promotes the release of endorphins, which helps fight stress and fatigue. Complementing classes with other techniques, for example, stretching, you can also improve flexibility, plasticity.

Who is the direction for

The dance was originally created not for professional performances, and therefore is suitable for beginner athletes, people who want to lose weight, and other categories of athletes. The zumba group in Almaty operates on the basis of the dance fitness studio Step Up. Workouts in the Latin American style are comparable to professional aerobics in terms of the degree of load. Therefore, there are some restrictions. Contraindications to classes Zumba should not be practiced by people with diseases of the musculoskeletal system and heart. There is also a time limit for trauma survivors who are in the process of rehabilitation. Pregnant women should also refrain from attending class throughout the pre-birth period.
