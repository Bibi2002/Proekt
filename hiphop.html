HIP-HOP

Hip hop has become arguably the most popular style for both girls and guys. It is danced in clubs and on city streets, this style can often be found in videos of famous musicians and singers, as well as in films. Recently, more and more films have been made about dancers, do you remember what a sensation the film "Street Dancing" made? From that moment on, hip-hop received a real boom in popularity among young people!

HIP-HOP DANCES

This dance style is a mixture of the dance cultures of the African and American continents. The history of hip-hop as a dance began in America in the poorest neighborhood of African Americans and Latinos, in the South Bronx in the early 1970s. Initially, this style was just entertainment for unemployed migrants, then it grew into a way to express their individuality, show themselves and take root in their dance style. More and more often, competitions began to be arranged between dancers, which subsequently began to gather a large audience, because it was very spectacular and incendiary.
