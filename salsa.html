How to learn to dance salsa?

In order to master this dance direction, you do not need either a young age or relevant experience on the floor. In salsa, you do not need to work out every movement, the experience is learned already on the dance floor! A couple of synchronized steps with a partner, several smooth movements of the hips, a smile, a mysterious look - and now you are already performing "salsa romance"! All that remains is to hone your skills by changing partners and communicating with them in a dance language. We are sure that salsa will give you real joy, and these sensations are difficult to compare with anything else.

Features of salsa

Like many other social trends, salsa is a flirting dance. At the same time, there are no permanent partners in it, this is entertainment for free and non-jealous natures. He leads, she follows and decorates. It is important to dance this dance easily and naturally, therefore, even in the process of learning salsa, it is customary to constantly change partners in order to learn to feel any “partner”. Experienced dancers can truly dance salsa with any partner, anywhere in the world, the first time. And it will look as if the couple spent hours rehearsing in the hall. Salsa is also considered a great avenue for self-improvement, both internally and externally. She perfectly develops creativity, the ability to improvise, teaches you to feel the rhythm and not be afraid of communicating with strangers. And this direction also requires a flexible body, good reaction and endurance. All this comes already in the learning process.
